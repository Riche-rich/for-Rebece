To My Better Half

- The Awakening -

My Dearest Rebeca, I am sitting here, reading your words over and over again, and I find myself struggling to keep my hands steady. You said that I am your favorite notification, but Rebeca, you are the heartbeat behind every second of my day. Reading what you wrote didn't just bring a smile to my face; it brought a calmness to my soul that I didn’t know I was missing until you came into my life.

Six months. It has been six months since we stood in front of our parents, our friends, and God, and made a promise that changed the trajectory of our entire existence. When I look back at that day, I realize that saying "Yes" to you wasn't just a decision—it was destiny finally catching up with me. You wrote that you would have regretted saying no, but my love, I don’t even want to imagine a universe where you aren't mine. That version of reality is too dark, too quiet, and too empty for me to comprehend.

❦

- The Reflection of You -

You listed so many things you love about me, but Rebeca, do you have any idea how much I admire you?

"You love the way I call you 'Maaa', but you don't see the look in your eyes when you answer me—that softness that melts my anger instantly."

"You love my sleepy voice, but I love the peace I feel knowing that you are the first thought in my mind when I wake up."

And yes... Mukku medha gudutha. You love when I say it, and I will keep saying it for the rest of our lives. It’s my way of saying that you are cute even when you are stubborn.

❦

- The Obsession -

You called me your obsession, but let me tell you about mine. My obsession is your happiness. My obsession is working hard at the gym so I can be strong enough to carry you—literally and emotionally—for the next sixty years.

When I hug you, I am trying to shield you from everything bad in this world. When I hold your hand, I am anchoring myself to the only thing that matters. From stranger to my main source of happiness... you own my heart completely.

❦

- The Vow of January -

January 22, 2025

That date isn't just a day on the calendar. It is the day my life truly begins. I am already making space for you in every closet, on every shelf, and in every empty moment of my day. I can't wait for grocery shopping with you. I can't wait to taste the food you cook with so much love.

I promise to be the man you deserve. I will be the irritation you secretly love, the shoulder you cry on, and the hand that pulls you up. I will honor you, respect you, and obsess over you every single day.

- Forever -

Words will always fall short. How do you explain the feeling of finding your home in a person? We have finished 6 months of this beautiful promise, but this is just the prologue.

Thank you for choosing me. Thank you for loving my imperfections. I love you not just yesterday, today, and tomorrow. I love you in every lifetime, in every universe, and in every heartbeat.

Your Husband
