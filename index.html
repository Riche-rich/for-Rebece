I Love You

REBECA

OPEN LETTER

My Dearest Wife, My Love,

I was sitting with your letter in my hand, reading your words over and over again. You said that there is so much "unsaid" in you, but reading this, I feel like I have looked directly into your soul. You have no idea what these words mean to me. You’ve left me speechless, breathless, and honestly, a little teary-eyed.

My love. It feels like just yesterday we were making those promises in front of our parents and friends, yet at the same time, it feels like I have known you for a lifetime. You are right—this journey hasn’t been a straight line. It has been a rollercoaster of everything. We have had our laughs, our romance, and yes, our "cold wars," our tears, and our misunderstandings.

But I want you to know something: I wouldn’t trade a single one of those difficult moments. Not one. Because every tear we wiped away and every misunderstanding we solved just brought us closer. It taught me how to love you better. It taught us how to hold onto each other when things get tough. You said that with the right person, everything seems good and right. Babe, you are that person for me. You are the one who makes the chaotic days peaceful and the quiet days full of life.

"You are the missing piece I didn’t know was gone."

♥

To the woman who loves my "Sleepy Voice",

I had to smile when I read your list. You love the way I irritate you? Good, because I have a lifetime of irritation planned for you! I love that we can be playful. I love that I can pull your cheeks and treat you like a kid sometimes, and you still look at me with that love in your eyes.

You mentioned “mukku medha gudutha”—and reading that made me laugh out loud. You better be careful, or I’m going to come find you right now just to annoy you until you say it again!

But let me tell you what I love:

  • ❤ I love the way you look at me when you think I’m not noticing.
  • ❤ I love the way you blush when I call you "Maaa."
  • ❤ I love that even when we fight, I can see that you still care about me.
  • ❤ I love how you have embraced my madness and matched it with your own.

You said I drive you crazy, but babe, you are the one who has made me obsessed. You are my beautiful obsession.

My Partner, My Family,

One of the things that touched my heart the most in your letter was when you wrote about being an "Amma like in-law" and your excitement to be part of my family.

I want you to know how much that means to me. A man’s biggest dream is to see his wife and his parents become one unit, one family. Seeing you accept them, and seeing how you want to cook for us and make a home with us, fills me with a pride I can’t explain. You haven't just married me; you have married into this house, and you have made it brighter just by being in it.

You wrote that you are excited to spend the rest of your life in my house and in my arms. I want you to know that my arms will always be your home. Wherever we are, as long as I am holding you, you are safe.

I am excited for your cooking (I’m already hungry thinking about it), but more than that, I am excited for the mundane moments. I’m excited to wake up next to you every day, to brush our teeth together, to watch TV, and to just be together. You are not just a guest in my life or my house; you are the queen of it.

VOW

To My "Thick and Thin",

You repeated the promises we made six months ago, and now I want to make them to you again, in writing, so you never forget.

I promise to stand by you.

When the days are dark and you feel like crying, I will be the shoulder you soak with your tears.

When you are sick, I will be the one by your bedside.

When you are happy, I will be the one celebrating the loudest.

Through the "cold wars" and the warm hugs, I am not going anywhere.

You said you found your man, and you found love. But I found my strength. You are my backbone. When I am weak, I look at you and find a reason to keep going.

I know I’m not perfect. I know I annoy you, I know I make mistakes. But my love for you is the most perfect thing about me. I will honor you, respect you, and love you every single second of my life.

My Forever Main Source of Happiness,

As I finish this letter, I feel like there is still so much I haven’t said. Words can’t really capture how you make me feel. You went from being a stranger to being the owner of my heart. It’s scary how much power you have over me, but I trust you with it completely. You are my best friend, my lover, my wife, and my future.

So, here is to us.

Here is to the next six months, the next six years, and the next sixty years.

Here is to more cheek pulling, more lip pulling, more driving each other crazy, and more love than we know what to do with.

Thank you for choosing me. Thank you for saying yes. Thank you for being mine.

I love you yesterday.

I love you today.

I love you tomorrow.

And I will love you forever.
